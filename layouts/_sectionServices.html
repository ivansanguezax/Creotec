Your Annual
Wellness Exam

At this appointment, you can generally expect your doctor to take your vital signs, including your blood pressure and heart rate, and check your eyes, ears, nose, and mouth. Finally, your doctor can determine whether you’re due for any immunizations, and also recommend routine screening tests that are appropriate for you.
